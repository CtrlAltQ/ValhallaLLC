About Valhalla Tattoo

Located in Spring Hill, TN, Valhalla Tattoo is a clean, professional, and welcoming studio offering high-quality custom tattoos. Our experienced artists bring a unique edge, rooted in tradition and elevated with modern techniques.

Tattoo answers, straight from the pros

Find essential info on booking, prep, safety, and aftercare.

How do I schedule an appointment?

Contact us or use our online booking. Reserve early for best availability.

How should I prepare?

Eat beforehand, stay hydrated, and wear accessible clothing.

What are your safety protocols?

We use single-use needles and follow strict sterilization standards.

How do I care for my tattoo?

Clean gently, avoid sun, and follow all aftercare instructions.
