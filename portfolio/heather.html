About Heather

Shop Manager & Apprentice

Heather is the heart of Valhalla Tattoo, serving as our Shop Manager while pursuing her passion as a tattoo apprentice.

As Shop Manager, Heather ensures everything runs smoothly, from scheduling appointments to maintaining our high standards of cleanliness and professionalism. Her organizational skills and attention to detail keep the studio operating at its best.

Currently training as an apprentice, Heather is learning the art of tattooing under the guidance of our experienced artists. Her dedication to both management and artistry makes her an invaluable part of the Valhalla team.
