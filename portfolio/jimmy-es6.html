About Jimmy

Jimmy has spent over 8 years perfecting the art of realistic tattooing, specializing in portraits, nature scenes, and detailed black and grey compositions. His meticulous attention to detail and understanding of light, shadow, and skin texture allows him to create tattoos that look like photographs. Each piece is carefully planned and executed over multiple sessions to achieve the highest level of realism.

My Process

Every realistic tattoo starts with a detailed consultation where we discuss your vision, reference materials, and placement. I create custom designs that work with your body's natural contours while maintaining the integrity of the original image. Multiple sessions allow for the layered approach that realistic tattooing requires.
